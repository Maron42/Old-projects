Norwegian - The tutorial

Verb quiz

Click "Start" to start the quiz. An English verb will appear in the upper textbox. Type the Norwegian translation of the verb in the box below (without the infinitive marker "å"), and click "OK". If your answer is correct, you'll score one point, if it's incorrect you'll loose one point. Then a new verb will appear. You win the game if you manage to score 10 points. You loose if you at any time during the game fall down to -4 points. Don't give up before you have won! If you want to start over again, click "Start". Good luck!
